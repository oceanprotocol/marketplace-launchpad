## Outcome

Your own fully functioning customised fork of Ocean Market deployed and working.

## Learning TIme 

90 minutes

## Description

This technical programming session will walk participants through the key steps to successfully fork and customize Ocean Market that are required to set up an Ocean powered data marketplace. Instruction covers cloning the market locally, installing dependencies, customizing branding elements and marketplace deployment. 

## Content

The module covers:

<Process>
Forking and running Ocean Market locally

Customising your fork of Ocean market

Quick deployment of Ocean Market
</Process>
