# Support

We aim to support you through your process of setting up and running your data marketplace. This support can be delivered both by the Ocean core team and our partners. Please feel free in get in touch if you would like to know more about how we can help you.

The following support areas are designed to provide opportunities to receive additional dedicated and personalized support to help you set up an Ocean powered marketplace. As always, community-lead support is available to everyone on Ocean's [Discord](https://discord.gg/TnXjkR5), [GitHub](https://github.com/oceanprotocol) and [Telegram](https://t.me/OceanProtocol_Community) channels.

## General Support

- We will stay in touch via Slack to support you and answer questions.
- In some situations we will offer ad hoc meetings to answer your general questions and help with your planning.

---

## Developer Support

- We will stay in touch via Slack to support you in your development phase.
- We are happy to assist with answering your questions and help with troubleshooting.
- In some situations we will offer ad hoc meetings to support you during critical development phases.
- We can support your deveops team through the launch and deployment phase.
